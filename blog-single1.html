The Art of Sushi: Tradition Meets Innovation at Hakato Kitaro Sushi

18 June 2025 by By Hakato Kitaro Sushi Team

Sushi is more than just food in Japan—it is an art form that balances simplicity, freshness, and tradition. At Hakato Kitaro Sushi, we embrace this philosophy by carefully selecting the finest ingredients each day and preparing them with respect for time-honored techniques.

Our counter service allows guests to watch the skillful hands of our chefs at work, creating sushi that is not only delicious but also a visual delight. From the delicate touch of slicing fresh fish to the precise roll of seaweed and rice, every step tells a story of dedication.

Blending Tradition with Creative Flavors

While we honor tradition, we also introduce creativity into our dishes. Signature items like the Kitaro Dragon Roll bring a modern twist, offering bold flavors that appeal to both locals and tourists. For those seeking a true culinary adventure, our Chef’s Omakase Platter showcases the very best seasonal selections, personalized for each guest.

Dining at Hakato Kitaro Sushi is not only about taste—it’s about atmosphere. Our cozy yet trendy space offers comfort for families, friends, and travelers, while our warm hospitality ensures every visit becomes a memorable experience.

Whether you’re visiting Fukuoka for the first time or are a local sushi lover, we invite you to join us and discover the perfect harmony of tradition and innovation at Hakato Kitaro Sushi.

The True Taste of Sushi in Fukuoka

  • Freshness First : We select only the best seasonal ingredients to ensure every bite is full of natural flavor.
  • Omakase Experience : Enjoy a chef’s choice menu designed to highlight the finest sushi of the day.
  • Creative Signatures : From classic nigiri to modern rolls, our dishes blend tradition with innovation.
Hakato Kitaro Sushi welcomes you to experience authentic Japanese dining in the heart of Fukuoka. From traditional sushi to chef’s specials, we take pride in serving fresh flavors with warm hospitality.
