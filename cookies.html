Cookies Policy

Effective Date: August 18, 2025

This Cookies Policy explains how Hakata Kitaro Sushi uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, enhance user experience, and analyze traffic.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly, such as enabling form submissions or page navigation.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect anonymous data about how visitors use our website, helping us improve performance and content.
  • Functionality Cookies: These remember your preferences, such as language or reservation details, for a better experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: These track browsing habits to provide relevant promotional content, but only if you opt-in.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our website may use third-party services (like Google Analytics) that set cookies to analyze website usage and performance. We do not have control over these third-party cookies, and we encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies using your browser settings. Please note that if you block or delete cookies, some features of our website may not function properly.

5. Your Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this Cookies Policy. You can withdraw your consent at any time by changing your browser settings.

6. Changes to This Cookies Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Please check this page periodically to stay informed.
