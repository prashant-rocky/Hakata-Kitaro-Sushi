Our story

From Ocean to Table, With Care

Hakato Kitaro Sushi was founded with a passion for preserving traditional Japanese sushi craftsmanship while embracing the rich flavors of Fukuoka’s seasonal ingredients. Every dish reflects dedication, precision, and artistry.

We believe sushi is more than just food—it’s an experience of culture, hospitality, and connection. At our restaurant, each guest is welcomed like family to share in that experience.
