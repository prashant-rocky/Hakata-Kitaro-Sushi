Effective Date: August 18, 2025

We collect personal information that you provide to us, including:

Your information is used for the following purposes:

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and analyze site traffic. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may affect certain functionalities of the site.
